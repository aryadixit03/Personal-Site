Intro

Hey There :)

Welcome to my profile.

My name is Arya and I am a second-year Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. I am a strong advocate for change, passionate about technology and innovation, and am a co-founder of my start-up, WearWave.

I love to innovate and integrate my passion for tech with other fields, such as business, project management, and social responsibility. See more about the work I have done with Deloitte, the National Bank of Canada, and more here.  Aside from work, I love to read, go on runs, bake, and sing. In fact, I have been learning Indian classical music for 14 years.

Additionally, in 2022 I received a great honour - the DMZ Women of the Year award. This award recognizes Canada’s most innovative women from diverse backgrounds and industries including startup founders, corporate leaders, non-profit trailblazers, and emerging youth. Having my name next to some of the most incredible female trailblazers in the tech industry continues to inspire me to make an impact in whichever way I can.

Feel free to contact me via email or on LinkedIn to connect further.

WearWave


   'Wear Better. Wear Safer. Wear Wave.'   


WearWave is a registered start-up that I co-founded with Masumi Vyas. We develop safe and customizable headphones designed for children ages 6-13. WearWave has worked with the well-known DMZ business incubator, the University of Western Ontario’s Morrissette Founder’s Institute, and the University of Waterloo’s Cornerstone Program.

See our Instagram: @wearwave.ca.
Check out our website for more on our products and team.
